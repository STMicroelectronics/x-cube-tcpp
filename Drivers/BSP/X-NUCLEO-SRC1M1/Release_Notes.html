Release Notes for X-NUCLEO-SRC1M1 BSP Driver

Copyright © 2021 STMicroelectronics

Purpose

The X-NUCLEO-SRC1M1 is an expansion board for the NUCLEO-G071RB and NUCLEO-G474RE development boards or any STM32 Nucleo development board equipped with a USB Type-C™ peripheral.

It provides a straightforward means for evaluating USB Type-C™ Power Delivery in SRC mode based on TCPP02-M18. The USB Type-C connector can supply the STM32 Nucleo development board thanks to a 3.3 V LDO.

Further information are available here:

https://www.st.com/en/ecosystems/x-nucleo-src1m1.html
